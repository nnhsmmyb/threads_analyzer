# Privacy Policy — threads_analyzer

**Last updated:** July 2, 2026  
**Contact:** taishi.oiwa@outlook.com

## Overview

threads_analyzer (“the App”) is a local command-line tool that helps a Threads user observe market topics related to career, remote work, and related themes. The App uses the [Threads API](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/threads) provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.

This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

## Who operates this App

The App is operated by the maintainer of the [threads_analyzer](https://github.com/nnhsmmyb/threads_analyzer) open-source project (“we”, “us”).  
The App is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Meta.

## What data we access

When you authorize the App through Threads OAuth, the App may access data from your Threads account according to the permissions you grant, including:

- Your Threads profile information (e.g. user ID, username)
- Your own Threads posts and media
- Public posts found via keyword search or public profile lookup
- Posts where you are @mentioned
- Replies on your posts
- Insights and analytics for your account and your posts
- Location data when you use location search or location tagging features

The App may also publish, reply to, delete, or manage content on your Threads account **only when you run it explicitly**.

## How we use data

We use the data solely to:

- Authenticate you with the Threads API
- Fetch and analyze Threads content for your personal market observation workflow
- Generate local reports (e.g. insight summaries) visible only to you
- Publish or manage content on your Threads account when you choose to do so

We do **not** use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

## Where data is stored

- **OAuth tokens** are stored locally on your device in a `.env` file.
- **Fetched content and reports** are stored locally in directories such as `agent_out/`, `input/`, and `t_data/` on your machine.
- We do **not** operate a central server that collects or stores your Threads data.

You are responsible for securing your device and your `.env` file.

## Data sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your Threads data with third parties.

Data is transmitted only between your device and Meta’s Threads API endpoints (`graph.threads.net`) as required for the App to function.

## Data retention

Data remains on your local machine until you delete it. You may remove fetched files and revoke the App’s access at any time.

## Your choices and rights

You can:

- **Revoke access:** Remove the App’s permissions in your [Threads account settings](https://www.threads.net/) (Website permissions) or in the Meta App Dashboard.
- **Delete local data:** Delete the App’s output folders and `.env` file from your computer.
- **Stop using the App:** Uninstall or stop running the App at any time.

If you are in a region with data-protection rights (e.g. GDPR), you may contact us at **taishi.oiwa@outlook.com** to ask questions about how the App handles your data.

## Children

The App is not intended for users under 13 years of age (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.
