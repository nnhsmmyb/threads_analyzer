# Privacy Policy — threads_analyzer

**Last updated:** July 2, 2026  
**Contact:** taishi.oiwa@outlook.com

## Overview

threads_analyzer is a sample local application that uses the [Threads API](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/threads). This policy describes how it handles data.

## Data we access

With your authorization, the App may access Threads data permitted by the scopes you grant (e.g. profile, posts, replies, insights). The App runs only on your device.

## How we use data

- Authenticate with the Threads API
- Display or process data for the authenticated user only
- Perform actions you explicitly trigger (e.g. fetch, publish, delete)

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

## Storage

OAuth tokens and output files are stored **locally** on your machine. We do not operate a server that collects your data.

## Your choices

You may revoke the App’s access in Threads or Meta account settings, delete local files, and stop using the App at any time.

## Third parties

The App communicates with Meta’s Threads API. Meta’s policies apply to data handled by Meta.
